BØRNS

BØRNS posing in front of anatomy artwork via BØRNS' Official Website

BØRNS is an indie pop, alternative rock, and electro pop musical artist. In 2012 he released his debut album, "A Dream Between". His 2015 album "dopamine" peaked at number 24 in the U.S., and was the catalyst to his success as a singer, songwriter, and instrumentalist. Specifically, his platinum certified song "Electric Love" was the highlighting piece that put his name on the charts for him to gain recognition. In 2018, he collaborated with Lana Del Ray on "God Save Our Young Blood", which charted on the U.S. rock chart.
